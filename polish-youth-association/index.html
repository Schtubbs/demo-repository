Empowering the Next Generation

The Polish Youth Association (PSM) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to strengthening the Polish-American community by empowering young leaders. Through our comprehensive programs, we create opportunities for cultural connection, professional development, and civic engagement.

Based in the heart of New York City, we serve as a bridge between Polish heritage and American opportunity, fostering leadership skills and cultural pride in the next generation of Polish-Americans.

Our Four Pillars

Comprehensive programs designed to connect, engage, explore, and amplify

🤝

Connect

Build meaningful relationships through our networking events, mentorship programs, and community gatherings that bring together Polish-American professionals and students.

💼

Engage

Gain real-world experience through volunteer opportunities and internship placements with leading organizations committed to community development.

🎓

Explore

Access educational opportunities through our scholarship programs and student club initiatives that support academic excellence and cultural learning.

📢

Amplify

Make your voice heard through community-building initiatives, advocacy programs, and leadership development opportunities.
