Building Bridges, Creating Futures

The Polish Youth Association (PSM) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to amplifying the voice of Polish-American youth across the nation. We create meaningful connections between young professionals, students, and community leaders while preserving our rich cultural heritage.

Through our comprehensive programs, we provide networking opportunities, volunteer placements, internships, scholarships, and community-building initiatives that empower the next generation of Polish-American leaders to thrive in their careers and communities.
