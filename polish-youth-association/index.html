Our Four Pillars of Impact

Comprehensive programs designed to nurture Polish-American youth from high school through professional success

🤝

Connect

Build meaningful professional and personal networks within the Polish-American community through exclusive events and mentorship programs.

  • Monthly networking events
  • Mentorship matching
  • Professional workshops
💼

Engage

Access exclusive volunteer opportunities and internship placements with Polish organizations and Fortune 500 companies.

  • Internship placements
  • Volunteer opportunities
  • Leadership development
🎓

Explore

Merit-based scholarships and student club support to help Polish-American students excel academically and culturally.

  • Academic scholarships
  • Student club support
  • Cultural exchange programs
📢

Amplify

Strengthen community bonds through cultural events, advocacy initiatives, and celebrating Polish heritage in America.

  • Cultural celebrations
  • Community advocacy
  • Heritage preservation
