A Family Tradition Since 1987

At Bella Rosa, we bring the authentic taste of Italy to Hackensack through three generations of family recipes. Every dish is prepared with the finest imported ingredients and the passion that only comes from true Italian heritage.

From our handmade pasta rolled fresh daily to our wood-fired pizzas baked to perfection, we create memorable dining experiences that transport you straight to the Italian countryside.

Our Specialties

🍝

Handmade Pasta

Fresh pasta made daily using traditional Italian techniques and recipes passed down through generations.

🍕

Wood-Fired Pizza

Authentic Neapolitan-style pizzas baked in our imported wood-fired oven for that perfect crispy crust.

🍰

Classic Desserts
